Capabilities

Core Capabilities

Semper Fi Services provides structured sourcing, documentation control, and compliant delivery support for aerospace, industrial, and government supply chains.

Aerospace & Defense Parts Distribution

Non-manufacturing distribution of aviation and industrial components sourced from approved manufacturers and authorized distributors, with strict adherence to technical and contractual requirements.

Traceability-Controlled Supply Chain Management

End-to-end control of sourcing, verification, documentation, and delivery, ensuring full accountability from supplier to end user.

Approved Source Procurement

Sourcing exclusively from verified manufacturers and authorized distributors to meet contract, engineering, and compliance requirements.

Documentation & Compliance Packages

Delivery of complete traceable documentation packages including Certificates of Conformance, inspection records, test data, and supporting compliance documentation.

FAR / DFARS Contract Compliance

Execution aligned with federal acquisition requirements, supporting structured communication, documentation control, and compliant delivery.

Packaging & Marking Control

Packaging, preservation, and labeling aligned with MIL-STD requirements and contract-specific shipping instructions.

Testing & Special Process Coordination

Coordination of third-party testing, certification, and special processes when required by technical data or contract clauses.

Inspection Readiness Verification

Pre-delivery validation of parts, documentation, and compliance requirements to support acceptance upon delivery.

Differentiators

Operational Experience Behind the Process

Semper Fi Services is built on direct experience in aviation quality assurance, maintenance documentation, inspections, and federal contract execution.

Aviation Quality Assurance & Maintenance Experience

Backed by 9 years of U.S. Marine Corps aviation experience, including over 5 years as a Quality Assurance Representative responsible for inspections, airworthiness documentation, compliance with technical directives, and maintenance oversight.

FAA-Certified Airframe & Powerplant Background

Direct experience reading maintenance manuals, interpreting technical publications, performing inspections, witnessing torques, and maintaining airworthiness documentation in regulated aviation environments.

Federal Contract Quality Control Experience

Experience supporting USACE and NAVFAC work with responsibility for inspections, Three-Phase Quality Control, submittals, RFIs, daily QC reporting, and contract documentation control.

Large-Scale Project Execution

Oversight and support experience on projects ranging from $1M to $80M+, including coordination of subcontractors, inspections, schedules, safety, and compliance requirements.

Safety, Risk, and Compliance Leadership

Background includes USACE/NAVFAC CQM-C, OSHA 30, EM 385-1-1, crane and rigging safety certifications, and direct experience in hazard mitigation, inspections, and high-risk operational environments.

Continuous Improvement Mindset

Lean Six Sigma exposure and process improvement experience supporting disciplined documentation, inspection readiness, and structured execution.
