Capabilities

Core Capabilities

Semper Fi Services provides execution-focused aerospace and industrial distribution, supporting DLA parts sourcing, NSN procurement, supplier verification, contract flowdown, traceability documentation, inspection readiness, and compliant delivery for government and defense supply requirements.

Government Supply Contract Execution

End-to-end support for federal and defense supply contracts, including sourcing, supplier coordination, documentation control, inspection requirements, packaging, and delivery tracking.

Supplier-Verified Procurement

Procurement from verified manufacturers, approved sources, and authorized distributors capable of supporting aerospace hardware, NSN parts, traceability, documentation, and inspection-ready contract requirements.

Contract Requirement Flowdown

Translation of FAR, DFARS, DLA, packaging, marking, testing, traceability, and inspection requirements into clear supplier expectations before commitment and release.

Production Lot Testing Coordination

Coordination of production lot testing requirements, sampling, lab interaction, test report review, and record control so material is not released before requirements are satisfied.

DCMA / QAR Source Inspection Readiness

Coordination of supplier readiness for government source inspection, including product status, document availability, traceability records, and contract requirement alignment before release.

Traceability & Documentation Control

Chain-of-custody tracking with controlled documentation packages including certificates of conformance, supplier records, test reports, inspection documents, and contract files.

MIL-STD Packaging & Marking Support

Packaging and labeling support aligned with MIL-STD-2073 preservation requirements, MIL-STD-129 marking expectations, shipment documentation, and acceptance readiness.

Inspection-Ready Delivery

Final verification of material, documentation, packaging records, shipment details, and contract requirements before release to support compliant delivery and acceptance.

Process

Process Overview

Our process is built around controlled government supply execution, ensuring every step from DLA sourcing and supplier verification through documentation, inspection readiness, packaging, and delivery is aligned with contract requirements.

This is not simple sourcing — it is a controlled execution system built to manage NSN parts procurement, supplier coordination, DCMA / QAR readiness, production lot testing, traceability records, MIL-STD packaging, and shipment release.
