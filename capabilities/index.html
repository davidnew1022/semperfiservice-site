Capabilities

Core Capabilities

Semper Fi Services provides execution-focused distribution, ensuring contracts are sourced, verified, documented, and delivered in full compliance with federal requirements.

Contract Execution & Distribution

End-to-end execution of federal supply contracts, managing sourcing, supplier coordination, inspection requirements, documentation, and delivery.

Supplier-Verified Procurement

Sourcing exclusively from validated manufacturers and authorized distributors capable of meeting contract requirements, including documentation and inspection readiness.

Contract Requirement Flowdown

Translation of contract clauses into executable supplier requirements, including inspection, testing, traceability, and packaging standards.

Production Lot Testing Coordination

Management of testing requirements including sampling, lab coordination, result validation, and documentation prior to shipment.

Source Inspection Readiness

Coordination of supplier readiness for government inspection, ensuring documentation, accessibility, and product compliance at time of review.

Traceability & Documentation Control

Full chain-of-custody tracking with complete documentation packages including CoC, test reports, and inspection records.

Packaging & Marking Compliance

Packaging and labeling aligned with MIL-STD-129 and MIL-STD-2073 requirements, ensuring acceptance upon delivery.

Inspection-Ready Delivery

Final verification of material and documentation before release, ensuring contracts are delivered complete, compliant, and ready for acceptance.

Process

Process Overview

Our process is built around contract execution, ensuring every step from sourcing through delivery is controlled, verified, and aligned with contract requirements.

This is not simple sourcing — it is a controlled execution system built to manage supplier coordination, inspection requirements, documentation, packaging, and delivery.

Quality & Compliance

Quality-Driven Execution Model

Quality is enforced through controlled processes, supplier verification, and strict documentation requirements aligned with AS9120 and ISO 9001 principles.

  • AS9120-aligned processes in development
  • Supplier verification and approval controls
  • Traceability and record retention systems
  • Inspection and documentation validation prior to delivery
  • FAR / DFARS-aware execution model

Quality Foundation

Our system is designed to ensure that no material is released without verification, complete documentation, and full alignment with contract requirements.

Differentiators

Operational Experience Behind the Process

Semper Fi Services is built on direct experience in aviation quality assurance, maintenance documentation, inspections, and federal contract execution.

Aviation Quality Assurance & Maintenance Experience

Backed by 9 years of U.S. Marine Corps aviation experience, including over 5 years as a Quality Assurance Representative responsible for inspections, airworthiness documentation, compliance with technical directives, and maintenance oversight.

FAA-Certified Airframe & Powerplant Background

Direct experience reading maintenance manuals, interpreting technical publications, performing inspections, witnessing torques, and maintaining airworthiness documentation in regulated aviation environments.

Federal Contract Quality Control Experience

Experience supporting USACE and NAVFAC work with responsibility for inspections, Three-Phase Quality Control, submittals, RFIs, daily QC reporting, and contract documentation control.

Large-Scale Project Execution

Oversight and support experience on projects ranging from $1M to $80M+, including coordination of subcontractors, inspections, schedules, safety, and compliance requirements.

Safety, Risk, and Compliance Leadership

Background includes USACE/NAVFAC CQM-C, OSHA 30, EM 385-1-1, crane and rigging safety certifications, and direct experience in hazard mitigation, inspections, and high-risk operational environments.

Continuous Improvement Mindset

Lean Six Sigma exposure and process improvement experience supporting disciplined documentation, inspection readiness, and structured execution.
