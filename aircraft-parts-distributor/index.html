Quality Approach

AS9120-Aligned Distribution Practices in Development

Semper Fi Services operates with a quality-first mindset built around supplier verification, documentation control, traceability review, contract requirement awareness, and disciplined release standards. While certification status must always be verified directly, our internal processes are being developed around AS9120 and ISO 9001 principles for aerospace distribution and quality management.

Our goal is to reduce sourcing risk by treating documentation, traceability, packaging requirements, and supplier communication as core parts of the procurement process.

  • Supplier capability reviewed before commitment
  • Traceability and documentation checked before shipment
  • Contract requirements reviewed during sourcing
  • Certifications and records organized for closeout
  • Packaging and labeling requirements considered before release
  • Nonconforming or unclear documentation flagged before delivery

Supplier Verification

Supplier Coordination Focused on Capability, Records, and Delivery

Supplier selection is one of the most important parts of aircraft parts distribution. Semper Fi Services reviews supplier capability, documentation readiness, responsiveness, and delivery feasibility before moving forward with sourcing support.

We prioritize suppliers that can provide clear communication, reliable lead times, accurate part information, required certifications, and traceability records when applicable.

Supplier Checkpoints

  • Manufacturer or distributor status
  • Part availability and lead time
  • Quality system or certification evidence
  • Traceability documentation capability
  • Packaging and shipment readiness
  • Responsiveness to RFQ requirements
  • Ability to support government or defense contract flowdowns

Documentation Control

Documentation Is Part of Delivery

For aircraft parts, military hardware, and government supply contracts, documentation is not an afterthought. Missing, incomplete, or incorrect records can delay acceptance, create inspection problems, or increase contract risk.

Semper Fi Services treats documentation as part of the product. We support organized review of supplier documents, certifications, traceability records, shipping evidence, and contract closeout materials.

  • Certificates of Conformance
  • Material certifications when required
  • Traceability records
  • Inspection or test documentation when applicable
  • Supplier quote and purchase records
  • Packaging and labeling evidence
  • Shipment tracking and delivery records
  • Contract closeout documentation
