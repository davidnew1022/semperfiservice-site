Quality System

Quality System Foundation

Our quality system is being built around AS9120-aligned aerospace distribution practices, supplier control, documentation accuracy, material traceability, chain-of-custody records, and disciplined execution so that government supply contracts can be sourced, verified, documented, packaged, and delivered with inspection-ready confidence.

AS9120 / ISO 9001-Aligned Distribution

Internal processes are structured around aerospace parts traceability, supplier verification, documentation control, record retention, and continuous improvement to support long-term AS9120 and ISO 9001 certification readiness.

Aerospace Supplier Verification

Supplier capability, authorized distribution status, documentation support, quality certifications, lead times, and execution readiness are reviewed before committing to DLA, NSN, defense, or federal supply requirements.

Documentation & Traceability Control

Certificates of conformance, supplier records, inspection documents, lot data, chain-of-custody records, and contract files are controlled so material can be traced, verified, and released with confidence.

Inspection-Ready Execution Discipline

Quality controls support consistent quote review, contract flowdown, source inspection coordination, MIL-STD packaging awareness, marking verification, delivery discipline, and lower execution risk from sourcing through shipment.
