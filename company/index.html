History

The Story Behind the Company

Semper Fi Services began with two Marines who carried a bigger vision for life after active service. Over time, that vision became grounded in real responsibility, long-term friendship, and a shared determination to build something meaningful for themselves and their families.

Through deployments, transition, work, and fatherhood, the friendship remained. What held it together was not just shared history, but a shared mindset: keep moving, stay accountable, solve problems, and build something that lasts.

Today, that same foundation shapes the company. Semper Fi Services is structured as a disciplined, execution-focused aerospace and government supply operation built around supplier verification, documentation control, traceability, process discipline, and contract-aligned performance.

Leadership

The Dynamic Duo Behind Semper Fi Services

Two complementary roles: Thomas leads supplier relationships, government-facing coordination, and field execution. David leads internal operations, technical verification, documentation control, traceability, and AS9120-aligned quality system development.

Thomas

The Face of the Company

Thomas brings a strong background in federal project execution, construction quality control, supplier coordination, and field operations. After serving in the United States Marine Corps from 2003-2007, and again from 2009-2015, he transitioned into construction and government project work where he gained direct experience supporting military installations, coordinating inspections, managing subcontractor activity, and maintaining accountability in demanding contract environments.

As Co-Owner of Semper Fi Services, Thomas leads supplier development, procurement coordination, and external operations. He is responsible for building relationships with manufacturers and distributors, supporting pricing and lead-time coordination, and helping ensure suppliers are prepared to meet documentation, inspection, and contract requirements.

Thomas and David bring different but complementary backgrounds together: field coordination, supplier relationships, and government-facing execution on one side; aviation quality assurance, documentation control, traceability, and internal operations on the other. That balance gives Semper Fi Services both the external reach and internal discipline needed to execute government supply work correctly.

David

The Behind-the-Scenes Operator

David is currently pursuing his education while building Semper Fi Services LLC from the ground up. He served 9 years in United States Marine Corps aviation, including over 5 years as a Collateral Duty Quality Assurance Representative.

As Co-Owner of Semper Fi Services, David leads internal operations, compliance, and quality system development. He is responsible for building and maintaining an AS9120-aligned Quality Management System, controlling documentation and traceability, and ensuring contracts are executed correctly from sourcing through delivery.
