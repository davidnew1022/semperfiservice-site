History

The Story Behind the Company

Semper Fi Services began with two Marines who both carried a bigger vision for life after active service. Over time, that vision became grounded in real-world responsibility, long-term friendship, and a shared determination to build something meaningful for themselves and their families.

Through deployments, transition, work, and fatherhood, the friendship remained. What held it together was not just shared history, but shared mindset: keep moving, stay accountable, solve problems, and build something that lasts.

Today, that same foundation shapes the company. Semper Fi Services is structured as a disciplined, execution-focused operation built to deliver reliable results in federal supply contracting through accountability, process control, and consistent performance.

What We Are Building

Semper Fi Services is being built as a contract execution company, not a traditional reseller. The focus is on controlling supplier coordination, documentation, and delivery to ensure contracts are completed correctly and consistently.

  • Execution-first contract selection
  • Supplier-controlled sourcing and coordination
  • Documentation and traceability discipline
  • Process-driven growth and scalability

Leadership

The Dynamic Duo Behind Semper Fi Services

The company is structured around two operational roles: external execution through supplier coordination, and internal execution through process control and compliance. Together, these functions ensure complete contract execution from sourcing through delivery.

Thomas

The Face of the Company

Thomas leads supplier development, procurement, and field coordination. He is responsible for building relationships with manufacturers and distributors, securing pricing and lead times, and ensuring suppliers are capable of meeting contract requirements.

His background in construction and quality control brings experience in managing execution, coordinating with external stakeholders, and maintaining accountability in performance-driven environments. He serves as the primary connection between Semper Fi Services and its supplier network.

David

The Behind-the-Scenes Operator

David leads internal operations, compliance, and quality system development. He is responsible for documentation control, traceability, receiving, packaging, and ensuring that all contract requirements are met before shipment.

With a background in military aviation and quality assurance, he brings a structured approach to process control, verification, and operational discipline. His focus is on building a system that ensures every contract is executed accurately, documented correctly, and delivered without error.
