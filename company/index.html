History

The Story Behind the Company

Semper Fi Services began with two Marines who both carried a bigger vision for life after active service. Over time, that vision became grounded in real-world responsibility, long-term friendship, and a shared determination to build something meaningful for themselves and their families.

Through deployments, transition, work, and fatherhood, the friendship remained. What held it together was not just shared history, but shared mindset: keep moving, stay accountable, solve problems, and build something that lasts.

Today, that same foundation shapes the company. Semper Fi Services is structured as a disciplined, execution-focused operation built to deliver reliable results in federal supply contracting through accountability, process control, and consistent performance.

What We Are Building

Semper Fi Services is being built as a contract execution company, not a traditional reseller. The focus is on controlling supplier coordination, documentation, and delivery to ensure contracts are completed correctly and consistently.

  • Execution-first contract selection
  • Supplier-controlled sourcing and coordination
  • Documentation and traceability discipline
  • Process-driven growth and scalability

Leadership

The Dynamic Duo Behind Semper Fi Services

The company is structured around two operational roles: external execution through supplier coordination, and internal execution through process control and compliance. Together, these functions ensure complete contract execution from sourcing through delivery.

Thomas

The Face of the Company

After serving in the United States Marine Corps from 2003-2007, and again from 2009-2015, Thomas transitioned back into civilian life through construction and federal project work. Over the years, that work has come with real personal cost, requiring long periods away from home while raising and providing for his four daughters as a single father. His experience includes working on military installations performing construction quality control management and consulting, coordinating inspections, and supporting execution on large-scale federal projects.

Today, as a Service-Disabled Veteran and Co-Owner of Semper Fi Services LLC, Thomas leads supplier development, procurement, and field operations. He works directly with manufacturers, coordinates on-site inspections, and ensures suppliers meet strict compliance and performance standards. His objective is to build a business that allows him to operate with discipline and accountability while creating consistent, reliable opportunity—both for his family and in support of U.S. warfighters. His long-term focus is on building something stable, scalable, and sustainable that creates a future his daughters can follow and be proud of.

David

The Behind-the-Scenes Operator

David is currently pursuing his education while building Semper Fi Services LLC from the ground up. He served 9 years in United States Marine Corps aviation, including over 5 years as a Collateral Duty Quality Assurance Representative. In that role, he was responsible for supervising maintenance, performing inspections, enforcing technical directives, and maintaining full documentation required for aircraft airworthiness. His experience includes working with technical publications, precision measuring tools, and managing quality programs in high-accountability environments.

As Co-Owner of Semper Fi Services, David leads internal operations, compliance, and quality system development. He is responsible for building and maintaining an AS9120-aligned Quality Management System, controlling documentation and traceability, and ensuring contracts are executed correctly from sourcing through delivery. He manages the contract pipeline, evaluates opportunities, and has developed the company’s technical infrastructure, including the website and internal systems. His objective is to build a disciplined, execution-focused operation that delivers consistent performance, supports long-term growth, and creates something stable and scalable for the future.
