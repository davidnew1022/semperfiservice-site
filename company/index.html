History

The Story Behind the Company

This page is about where Semper Fi Services comes from, what shaped it, and why the company is being built the way it is.

Semper Fi Services began with two Marines who both carried a bigger vision for life after active service. Over time, that vision became grounded in real-world responsibility, long-term friendship, and a shared determination to build something meaningful for themselves and their families.

Through deployments, transition, work, fatherhood, and personal struggles, the friendship remained. What held it together was not just shared history, but shared mindset: keep moving, stay accountable, solve problems, and build something that lasts.

Today, that same foundation shapes the company. Semper Fi Services is being built as a disciplined, trustworthy operation that reflects military values, personal resilience, and a serious commitment to doing business the right way.

What We Are Building

We are building a company that combines personal grit with operational discipline. The goal is not just to win work, but to create a dependable, quality-focused business that buyers, manufacturers, and partners can trust.

  • Mission-oriented execution
  • Long-term relationships over short-term shortcuts
  • Professionalism rooted in real-life experience
  • Growth built on discipline, not hype

Leadership

The Dynamic Duo Behind Semper Fi Services

The company reflects two different strengths working together: one outward-facing, one operationally focused, both committed to the same mission.

Thomas

The Face of the Company

Thomas brings leadership presence, relationship-building strength, and practical field experience to the company. He represents the visible, forward-facing side of Semper Fi Services: the man who helps communicate the mission, build confidence, and carry the company’s values into the marketplace.

His background in construction and quality control brings real-world discipline to how work is approached. He understands accountability, responsibility, and what it means to represent a company with credibility.

David

The Behind-the-Scenes Operator

David drives the internal structure of the company: systems, documentation, process, strategy, and the details that make execution dependable. He is focused on building the foundation that allows the business to scale with discipline and professionalism.

With a military aviation background and quality assurance experience, he brings a mindset built around precision, traceability, and operational control. He is the one making sure the back end is as strong as the front.
