History

The Story Behind the Company

Semper Fi Services began with two Marines who both carried a bigger vision for life after active service. Over time, that vision became grounded in real-world responsibility, long-term friendship, and a shared determination to build something meaningful for themselves and their families.

Through deployments, transition, work, and fatherhood, the friendship remained. What held it together was not just shared history, but shared mindset: keep moving, stay accountable, solve problems, and build something that lasts.

Today, that same foundation shapes the company. Semper Fi Services is structured as a disciplined, execution-focused aerospace and government supply operation built to deliver reliable results through supplier verification, documentation control, traceability, process discipline, and contract-aligned performance.

What We Are Building

Semper Fi Services is being built as a contract execution and distribution company, not a simple reseller. The focus is on controlling supplier coordination, aerospace hardware sourcing, documentation, traceability, packaging awareness, and delivery so government supply contracts are completed correctly and consistently.

  • Execution-first DLA and federal supply contract selection
  • Supplier-controlled sourcing and manufacturer coordination
  • Documentation, traceability, and chain-of-custody discipline
  • AS9120-aligned growth path and scalable operating systems

Leadership

The Dynamic Duo Behind Semper Fi Services

The company is structured around two complementary operational roles: Thomas leads supplier relationships, government-facing coordination, and field execution, while David leads internal operations, FAA A&P-informed technical verification, documentation control, traceability, and AS9120-aligned quality system development. Together, these functions support complete contract execution from sourcing through delivery.

Thomas

The Face of the Company

Thomas brings a strong background in federal project execution, construction quality control, supplier coordination, and field operations. After serving in the United States Marine Corps from 2003-2007, and again from 2009-2015, he transitioned into construction and government project work where he gained direct experience supporting military installations, coordinating inspections, managing subcontractor activity, and maintaining accountability in demanding contract environments.

As Co-Owner of Semper Fi Services, Thomas leads supplier development, procurement coordination, and external operations. He is responsible for building relationships with manufacturers and distributors, supporting pricing and lead-time coordination, and helping ensure suppliers are prepared to meet documentation, inspection, and contract requirements. His objective is to build Semper Fi Services into a disciplined, reliable, and scalable government supply partner focused on strong supplier relationships, accountable execution, and long-term contract performance.

David

The Behind-the-Scenes Operator

David is currently pursuing his education while building Semper Fi Services LLC from the ground up. He served 9 years in United States Marine Corps aviation, including over 5 years as a Collateral Duty Quality Assurance Representative. In that role, he was responsible for supervising maintenance, performing inspections, enforcing technical directives, and maintaining full documentation required for aircraft airworthiness. His experience includes working with technical publications, precision measuring tools, and managing quality programs in high-accountability environments.

As Co-Owner of Semper Fi Services, David leads internal operations, compliance, and quality system development. He is responsible for building and maintaining an AS9120-aligned Quality Management System, controlling documentation and traceability, and ensuring contracts are executed correctly from sourcing through delivery. He manages the contract pipeline, evaluates opportunities, and has developed the company’s technical infrastructure, including the website and internal systems. His objective is to build a disciplined, execution-focused operation that delivers consistent performance, supports long-term growth, and creates something stable and scalable for the future.
